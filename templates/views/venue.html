Tasty munch

Herb crusted kingklip on a bed of lemon crushed potatoes, green vegetables, onion soubise and saffron sabayon. Depending on the season, that's the kind of thing they'll be serving. Seriously.

We chose the Clico because they take food seriously, no matter the occasion. On the day, you can expect: "On arrival" refreshments, Mid morning and afternoon tea and snacks and a 2 course lunch “menu of the day” prepared by the resident chef.

Free, unlimited wi-fi

You'll be able to connect you laptops, tablets, phablets and phones to an unlimited dedicated wi-fi network all day. This'll be important for accessing all the awesome Axure resources on the web that we'll be showing you.
