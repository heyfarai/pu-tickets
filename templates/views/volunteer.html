Help make PIXEL UP! awesome.

If you’re a student and want to attend PIXEL UP! 2016 but can’t manage to purchase a ticket, you can join our student volunteer program and help support one of the best digital design events and attend for free.

Registration will be open until August 14th 2016. We will process applications as quickly as possible and get back to you in the week of August 15th, 2016.

What will you do as a volunteer?

As a volunteer, you’ll be expected to dedicate at minimum 8 hours working shift to the event. You’ll be also responsible for any travel and accommodation costs to, during and from the conference. You be doing stuff like:

  • Putting together swag bags
  • Managing the registration desk and checking people in
  • Monitoring the conference rooms and help set up audio-visual equipment
  • Supporting social events (parties etc.)
  • Acting as “floaters” - walking around the conference venue and answer participants’ questions and guiding them around the venue.
  • Helping out at lunch time
  • Helping speakers
  • Running around with microphones during Q&A sessions
  • Moving people around
  • General administration

What you’ll get

You will get an exclusive insider’s view to Africa’s premier digital design conferences. It’ll be unforgettable and will include:

  • An inside look at the planning stages of the conference
  • Free conference attendance for all 3 days including talks and some workshops
  • Free attendance to parties
  • Meals covered on all the days you volunteers
  • The opportunity to meet rad product thinkers designers and makers
  • An exclusive, volunteer-only PIXEL UP! T-shirt
  • Kudos for your time, attention and efforts

We are unable to cover expenses for volunteers, which means that if you want to get involved, you will need to cover the costs of travelling to, and staying in Johannesburg.

When do you have to be available?

You have to be available starting on Sunday, September 11th until Wednesday, September 14th 2016. Start times depend on the day - volunteers may start on some days as early as 7 am and end as late as 9 pm on other days. We do our best to make sure that you get the rest you need, but be prepared for an intense few days!

APPLY to volunteer

No qualifications necessary! You must however show proof that you are currently studying somethin related to design or product management. It helps to have a good attitude, act organised and feel enthusiastic about helping others... and of course have an interest in the making products with pixels.
