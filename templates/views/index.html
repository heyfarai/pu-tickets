#PixelUp

A 3 Day Digital Product Design Conference in Johannesburg, South Africa

We bring engaging industry leaders from around the world to present a combination of inspirational talks and lively, hands-on workshops.

Its the first event of it's kind in Africa. Its an event for digital product designers and developers to meet, learn and get inspired about making products customers will love.

3 days. 12 experts. 3 workshops.

Pixel Up! happens over 3 days. Day 1 and Day 2 are full conference days, with one track, 12 experts from the industry and 200 fantastic attendees. Day 3 is packed with 3 full-day workshops delivered by local and international experts.
