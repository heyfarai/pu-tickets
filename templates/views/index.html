3 days. 12 experts. 3 workshops.

Day 1 and Day 2 are full single track conference days, where you'll get lots of ideas and inspiration for talks by our 12 industry experts. On Day 3 you'll go beyond inspiration into learning practical skills with 3 full-day workshops delivered by the experts. It's a great day out for teams who want to work together more closely.

Come to Pixel Up! if you:

  • Design apps and/or websites
  • Build websites using the latest HTML5, JS and CSS tech
  • Build Native apps for phones, tablets and wearables (TV)
  • Manage projects that create apps and websites
  • Hire designers
  • Hire web and native app developers
  • Manage (or pay for) medium to large web sites
  • Manage (or pay for) iOS, Android and Windows apps
  • Just wanna hang out with some geeks and designers
