Day Three Wednesday, Sept. 14

Day 3 is workshop day. Go beyond inspiration and immerse yourself in groundbreaking design & development skills you won’t find anywhere else. Get your team on the same page with proven methods, tools and clear-cut direction on specific actions and skills.

Once the schedule is complete we'll update this page. Stay in touch to make sure you get one of our limited workshop seats.

Full day workshops include:
  • Snacks and drinks all day
  • Lunch
You should bring:
  • Your laptop
  • Your laptop charger
Workshop tickets

Tickets for the one day workshops are R2,500.00 (including VAT). Workshop tickets are valid for the workshop only. To attend the conference days an extra ticket is needed.
All workshops start at 9:00am and end at 4:00pm.

{#
  1. 9:00am - 12:30pm
    What Video Games Teach Us About UX

    Video games teach us the value of writing content before an interface exists, how to apply content-first UX design, and why voice and tone is vital to engagement. Explore the broad-to-narrow flow for introducing new content or interactions.

    Beyond Measure: Making Meaning From Data

    There’s lots of data we can track, measure, and store, but it doesn’t mean it creates more meaning. Hear the the danger of making decisions based on emotion and superstition. Learn strategies for decision-making that bridge the divide between left- and right-brain thinking.
